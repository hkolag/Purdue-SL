Objective

Team Objective

The Purdue Student Launch Team, started in 2011, has grown into an advanced and forefront contender in the NASA USLI competition. With members from various majors and backgrounds, Purdue SL boasts a diverse setting for teammates to not only provide the team with valuable ideas, but also offer the newer members a chance to get involved in the various aspects of the project, including avionics, design, payload, educational outreach, and social ventures.
