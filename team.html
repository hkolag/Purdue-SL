Avionics Head

"I supervise important components such as GPS tracking, telemetry and the exact time that our parachutes are released. Josh Fitch

Educational Engagement Head

"My goal is to coordinate and organize outreach events for students K-12 in the Lafayette. Our goal us to reach 200 students and teach them STEM related topics by using hands on activities such as stomp rockets for the younger elementary and middle school kids, and basic rocketry concepts for the older high school age kids."
