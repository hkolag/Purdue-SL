Meet Our Team


Victor Barlow

Faculty Mentor

"As team mentor, my role is to advise the team with regard to the design, construction, launch and recovery of both rockets. I monitor compliance with all safety rules and procedures, assist in educational outreach and fund raising, and supervise all team activities."


Micheal Repella

Project Manager

"I make sure everything in the project is falling into place as it should. I ensure communication is flowing well between teams and that everyone has what they need to do their job. I also delegate tasks to the sub-teams, organize and carry out meetings, and work to resolve any problems that may arise within the team."


Luke Perrin

Assistant Project Manager

"My responsibility is to serve as a liaison between the Project Manager and sub team leads. I plan agendas and lead weekly meetings to coordinate and facilitate efforts amongst individuals."


Reni Patel

Avionics Head

"Our team manages the avionics bay including altimeters, batteries, wiring, parachute and ejection methods. We will be conducting research on altimeters and design our own for use in subscale testing and propose the idea to NASA"


Zach Carroll

Construction Head

"I am responsible for building both the subscale and fullscale rockets. Having a dedicated team allows us to focus on making sure the rocket is as well-built as possible. In the meantime, our team focuses on helping out the other teams to make sure all technical documents are above and beyond what's required."


Sean Heapy

Funding Head

"I am in charge of funding all expenses for the team, as well as managing all business-related team matters. I am currently working on organizing and scheduling local fundraising events such as skip-a-meals. I also look forward to funding the majority of the team's expenses through non-profit grants and company sponsorships."


Wes O'Dell

Payload Head

"I am responsible for overseeing the successful completion of our payload system. More specifically I make decisions about the hardware that will be used, and develop software for our system."


Jory Lyons

Safety Head

"I am responsible for enforcing and creating guidelines and policies to establish a safe work environment to prevent accidents from happening. These goals are accomplished by utilizing detailed risk analysis tables, step-by-step procedures on how to accomplish various tasks related to the project, and checklists to use on launch day, among other things."


Harith Kolaganti

Social Head

"The main task for me this year was the create this website from scratch, as well as the social media outlets. I run and maintain these sites on weekly basis in order to spread the exposure of our team around campus to bring in more new members. Aside from these aspects, I am also involved in helping the fundraising team recieve grants and other forms of donations."
